About my project

I have worked on my GT project about Gmail and how it works. I have explained the process of sending email from person to person, uncovered information about Gmail history, and have shone light on this free, widely used service from Google.
Here is a brief intro of the topics from my presentation. To learn more about these topics, watch the video below.

What is Email?

Email is short for electronic mail. It is a virtual version of traditional post and mail like USPS. It is used for communication and document sharing.

How Gmail works?

A message in Gmail takes a wild journey through servers, protocols, and computers. To learn more about this wondorous way of communicating, please watch the video.

History of Gmail

Paul Buccheit was a Google employee who worked on the concept of email. Google launched Gmail on April 1, 2004. Gmail has continued to evolve and adapt ever since its release.

Safe email practices

We should follow some safety rules for emails to protect our private information, like passwords and credit card numbers, from being stolen. It also keeps malware away from your device.
